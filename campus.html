LIBRARY


Merchiston Library is a bright attractive environment providing over 500 study spaces designed to suit a variety of learning styles. They also have a help desk with staff, eager to help any way they can!

The Merchiston Library offers a large selection of books on Gender, Identity and Sexuality. These can be read while within the library, or borrowed to read at your own pace.

STUDENT HUB


Napier's counsellors are here to listen, empathise and support you. They are a friendly and approachable trained professionals with a number of tools to help you find the right coping mechanisms that work for you.

They aim to help you find a way forward when it feels like the world is getting you down.

Offered services are Counselling Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Mental Health Advice, Mindfulness, Wellbeing Advice and Mindfulness Drop-ins

ENSA


Napiers very own student association! If you've heard or been to any of Napier's events its no doubt ENSA's doing ! This group of hard working individuals make it their priority to make the student experience an engaging and fun one, while helping out with any issues that they can.

From free, confidential advice, to Sports and Societies and even helping with Duke of Edinburgh awards. ENSA are your go to for all things student life around Napier. You can even get involved by becoming part of the team during elections!
