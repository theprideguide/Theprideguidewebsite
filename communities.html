Communities


Throughout Edinburgh, there are many places to find LGBT+ communities. Whether these are full of people showing support or other members of the community, when you know where to look, there are countless groups to be found.

Communities such as Queer Edinburgh. A group for finding friends and events in Edinburgh. Plus the many more than can found through databases such as LGBT+ Youth's or Stonewalls

Within Napier there are groups such as Queer Napier that provide a welcoming atmosphere for anyone looking to join or find out more about their group.
