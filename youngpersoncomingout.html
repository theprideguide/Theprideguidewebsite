Coming out as a young person


About coming out young: The Pride Guide was established in 2020 to combine
digital safe spaces with safe locations on Napier Campus' for LGBT+ students.

Info about website where info is found: The aim is that these safe spaces would allow students
to go and find out information and support in their
own time at their own space, without fear of judgement.

Download/link to info: Everyone is welcome to visit the event to find out further information or even meet up with fellow LGBT+ students!
