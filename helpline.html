Helplines


Ever need someone to talk to? We all need a little help sometimes and theres no shame in reaching out to those happy and willing to help. When friends or family may not know what to say there are people out there that will try their best.

LGBT+ Helpline Scotland: 0300 123 2523, Open every Tuesday and Wednesday between 12 - 9pm.
24hr services such as the Samaritans are also available.

Don't want to talk? Sometimes it can be nerve wracking to speak to someone new. There are email or live chat services however, such as LGBT+ Scotland who can be emailed here: helpline@lgbthealth.org.uk or their Tuesay/Wednesday livechat can be found Here
