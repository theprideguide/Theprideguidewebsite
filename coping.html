Coping Methods


The LGBT community is vulnerable to stress in ways that most are often not. Commonly known as “minority stress” this can be triggered by a variety of factors from outright verbal/physical abuse or discrimination to the internalised feeling of not belonging or being accepted.

While localised in Manchester, the LGBT+ Foundation provides a guide to help suggest some coping methods that they have to help to ease discomfort that can be applicable to all.
