About Pride Guide


The Pride Guide is a student created project which aims to bring safe spaces for LGBT+ students to campuses. To establish a place to go for students to learn and meet up with other students in their own time in an environment where they donâ€™t feel pressured.

We hope to help you find somewhere to destress and feel at ease on campus. While the space can be accessed by anyone, resources and information provided will cater specifically towards the LGBT+ community. This project was created with the thought that groups and societies could meet in this safe space also, and feel like their community can have a go to location to meet up.
