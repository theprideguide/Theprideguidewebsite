COMING OUT


"People often assume I am straight. In the instance when they ask about my husband, I have to evaluate whether me telling them I have a wife will impact the choices they will make." - Woman, gay.

Coming out can be an overwhelming, confusing time but you're not alone. There are thousands of others out there in the LGBT+ community in just Scotland itself.

IDENTITY


Did you know that out of the 108,100 people who took part in the national LGBT+ survey Sixty one percent of respondents identified as gay or lesbian and 26% identified as bisexual?

Identity is something deeply personal, and something that we tend to struggle with more than anything else.
