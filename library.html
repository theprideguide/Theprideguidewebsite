About The Library


Merchiston library is a quiet, calm space where students can always be found researching or working on their classwork! Zones 1 and 2 offer social study space with Group Study Pods and circular tables for group work, Zone 3 is our silent room, Zones 4 and 5 offer quieter study space around the book stock. There is a wide variety of material which supports the diverse subjects taught on campus and more.

For further information check out their page here!.

Zones 1 - 4 are open 24/7 however, Zone 5 and the help desk are open 8:45am - 6:00om Monday to Friday
