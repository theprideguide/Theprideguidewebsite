ABOUT THE SAFE SPACE

The Pride Guide Pop up is a safe, quiet space to sit and relax in a calm, quiet enviroment. A range of resources can be found and looked through at your own pace. This includes information booklets with official LGBT+ Youth information, as well as a further guide to safe places around campus.

A range of LGBT+ books will also be on display nearby. These include topics such as Gender, Identity and Sexuality. If there are any books you are interested in, but not available simply let us know and we will work on adding these to the library!

Location

Currently located at the Napier Merchiston Campus. The Safe place is situated in the Merchiston library top floor. This can be accessed past the maind room group study areas and to the left. Following the stairs upwards then head left/right. Signs will guide the way to ensure you don't get lost, or ask one of our friendly Librarians for directions if you're stuck!

Opening hours: Monday - Friday during staffed hours: Nine to Six.

Address: 10 Colinton Rd, Edinburgh EH10 5DT
